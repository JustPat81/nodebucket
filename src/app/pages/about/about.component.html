Nodebucket

Welcome to the newly developed Nodebucket web application. This application has been in the works for over a year and we are pleased to finally make it available to the public. This is the first application of many to come from the JustP@ Development Group. We hope you enjoy this new app which is designed to help keep your tasks organized.
Nodebucket is a task organization app that is designed to, you guessed it, keep you organized and on track. It allows you to add tasks to the To Do list, and reorganize them by dragging and dropping. When you finish a task you can move it over to the Done column by dragging and dropping as well. The drag and drop feature is an excellent way to reorganize your tasks as you add them to the lists. Another great feature of Nodebucket is the ability to delete a task. The delete option offers a great way to erase any task that is no longer needed on the To Do list or the Done list. Be careful with this feature as it is permanent and cannot be undone.
We really hope you do enjoy the Nodebucket application and all it has to offer.
